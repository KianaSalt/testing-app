Bangkok Tourist Attractions

A Beauty named The Grand Palace

A royal compound with incredible structures that are beautiful. This place was built in 1782 and is still used for important ceremonies. Remember to dress modestly while visiting the Grand Palace.

Shop at the Damnoen Saduak Floating Market

A market that you can shop at by boat. Bangkok used to have the nickname “Venice of the East” because of the high demand for floating markets. You will be able to buy fresh and delicious food. Also, connect with the locals. The best way to experience this is to book a tour with a company.

Punta Cana Tourist Attractions

Monkeyland

An animal sanctuary located in the Punta Cana jungle. A group of adorable squirrel monkeys home and they’ve been trained to be safe and interactive around humans. Also, you can sample local Dominican coffee.

Visit the Chocolate Museum

The Dominican Republic is famous for its chocolate and cacao, so they have a museum dedicated to the delicious natural treat. You will learn their history of chocolate and even make your own chocolate. The museum is free but with activities that charge.
